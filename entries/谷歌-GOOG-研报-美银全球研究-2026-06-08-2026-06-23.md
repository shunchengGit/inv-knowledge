Web traffic: Claude and Meta AI have best m/m growth 
Per Similarweb, May global daily avg. web visits to Google were up 5% y/y to 2.8bn, 
ChatGPT was +2% y/y to 180mn, Gemini was +451% y/y, Claude was +857% y/y, and 
Meta AI was +105% y/y.  On m/m basis, Google was flat, ChatGPT was down 2%, Gemini 
was +2%, Claude was +14%, and Meta AI was +17%.  In the US, Web visits to Google 
were +2% y/y to 535mn, ChatGPT was +25% y/y to 32mn (6% of Google), Gemini was 
+289% y/y, Claude was +760% y/y, and Meta AI was +42% y/y. On m/m basis, Google 
was down 2%, ChatGPT was -7%, Gemini was -5%, Claude was +2%, & Meta AI +2%. 
Mobile DAUs: Gemini and ChatGPT add 10mn users m/m 
In May, per Sensor Tower, Google global mobile DAUs increased 12% y/y (+1% m/m) to 
2.2bn, ChatGPT was up 68% y/y to 438mn (+2% m/m), Gemini up 321% y/y to 110mn 
(+10% m/m), Claude +1,349% to 17mn (+22% m/m), Meta AI +1,321% to 10mn (+25% 
m/m), & Perplexity +67% y/y to 8m. In the month of May, Gemini added 10mn DAUs 
m/m, ChatGPT also added 10mn, Claude added 3mn while Meta AI added 3mn.  
Search share: Google global share marginally up y/y 
Per Statcounter, Google’s global search share was up 44bps m/m vs. Bing down 16bps 
m/m. On a y/y basis, Google’s market share was up 89bps y/y to 90.5% while Bing’s 
gained 100bps y/y to 5.0%, and Others were down 195bps y/y to 3.2%. In the US, 
Google’s search market share was up 66bps m/m to 85.8% (down 111bps y/y). 
Claude and Gemini growth strong, Google trends stable 
In May, Google traffic & user growth was largely stable while ChatGPT grew but decelerated 
on a y/y basis, while Claude, Gemini and Meta AI continued to gain traction. We think the 
new ad formats Google announced at Marketing Live should help capitalize on growing AI 
search format usage (1bn users of AI Mode, Gemini at 900mn MAUs) and could accelerate 
Search monetization. Maintain Buy on Alphabet. Also, data suggests growing engagement 
on the standalone Meta AI app, as Meta utilizes its large user base to drive adoption of new 
surfaces. Maintain Buy on Meta. Next Google usage catalysts: Broader availability of Gemini 
3.5 Pro and rollout of new agentic search features announced at I/O.
