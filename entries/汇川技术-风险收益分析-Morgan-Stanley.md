Reason for change
We adjust our forecasts following 4Q25 and 1Q26 earnings, and introduce our 2028 
forecasts.
Our revenue forecasts are revised up 4%/5% for 2026/27, respectively, to reflect 
stronger growth in the automation business and emerging business (incl. intelligent 
robot and digital energy), on the back of the automation upcycle and ongoing 
market share gain.
Our EPS estimates are revised down 3% for 2026, mainly reflecting lower 
profitability from the NEV business and FX loss, but are up 3% for 2027 on 
continuous cost pass-through and a better revenue mix.
We use SOTP for our Inovance valuation: 1) apply a 35x P/E (unchanged) to 2026e 
EPS for automation business; 2) NEV powertrain is based on its market cap and 
Inovance's 83% stake; 3) emerging business – we apply 60x P/E to 2026e EPS, in-
line with the 60x we gave for Estun's core industrial robot business; and 4) we apply 
5x P/S (unchanged) for the humanoids business when global humanoid sales reach 
1mn units. Our PT stays unchanged at Rmb95.0.
