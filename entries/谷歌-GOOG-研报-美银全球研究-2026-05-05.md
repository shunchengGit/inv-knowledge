Traffic: Gemini +9% m/m, ChatGPT -1%, Claude +33% 
Per Similarweb, April global daily avg. web visits to Google increased 4% y/y to 2.8bn, 
ChatGPT was up 7% y/y to 183mn, Gemini was up 572% y/y to 92mn, and Claude was up 
745% y/y to 27mn.  On m/m basis, Google traffic was up 1%, ChatGPT was down 1%, 
Gemini was up 9% and Claude was up 33%.  US web visits to Google were up 2% y/y to 
546mn, ChatGPT was up 30% y/y to 34mn (6% of Google), Bing was down 6% y/y to 
44mn. On m/m basis, Google traffic was up 1%, ChatGPT was up 1%, Claude was up 
28%, and Gemini was up 8%.   
DAUs: Gemini +13% m/m, ChatGPT flat, Google +1%  
In Apr, per Sensor Tower, Google global mobile DAUs increased 12% y/y (+1% m/m) to 
2.2bn, ChatGPT +88% y/y to 429mn (flat m/m), Gemini +336% y/y to 101mn (+13% 
m/m), Claude +1,252% to 14mn (+41% m/m), Perplexity +104% y/y to 9mn, & Grok was 
at 11mn.  In Apr, Gemini added 11mn DAUs m/m, ChatGPT was +2mn, and Claude +4mn. 
Search share: Google global share marginally up y/y 
Per Statcounter, Google’s global search share was up 7bps m/m vs. Bing up 1bp m/m. On 
a y/y basis, Google’s market share was up 37bps y/y to 90.0% while Bing’s gained 
125bps y/y to 5.1%, and Others were down 179bps y/y to 3.3%. In the US, Google’s 
search market share was up 16bps m/m to 85.2% (down 155bps y/y), while Bing was 
down 3bps m/m and up 232bps y/y to 9.8%. 
Claude and Gemini traction continues, Google still growing 
In Apr, Google traffic and user growth was largely stable while ChatGPT growth has 
decelerated, possibly due to growing traction of Claude & Gemini. We think traffic data 
continues to validate our thesis that AI is driving an expansionary search usage cycle.  
On top of strong usage, we expect AI integrations to aid search monetization through 
improved intent understanding and higher monetization of zero-click queries, while 
agentic capabilities could enhance performance and measurement. Maintain Buy on 
Google.  Next Catalysts: I/O Conf (May 19), upgraded Gemini model launch, and optimism 
on Gemini monetization, include agentic launches. Risk is relatively elevated valuation (vs 
recent history) and new competitive product launches (GPT-6, OpenAI ad ramp, etc.).
