Macquarie Equity Research
28 April 2026
Inovance
NEVs to weigh on FA demand recovery
Key Points
• 4Q25 and 1Q26 below MQe on weak revenue growth and expense hikes.
• We expect China FA demand to bottom out. Inovance is the key
proxy. However, we expect slowing NEV to drag down its revenue and
earnings.
• Trough valuations but mixed outlook. We lower TP 10% to Rmb77.80.
•
What we liked: 4Q25/1Q26 GPM remained steady at 28.2%/29.1%.
•
What we did not like: 4Q25 SG&A and R&D expense increased by 49.7%
and 34.0% you, respectively. 1Q26 operating CF saw a sharp decline YoY/
QoQ.
•
Industrial automation revenue/order mismatch due to capacity
bottlenecks. Inovance reported 1Q26 industrial automation
revenue growth of 14% YoY, below order book growth of 40%, which
management attributed to capacity bottlenecks. Following a 30%
capacity expansion, Inovance expects 2Q26 FA revenue growth to align
with its order book. We believe China FA demand will bottom in 2026.
We expect Inovance to continue gaining market share from global peers,
supporting industrial automation revenue growth above the industry.
•
NEV segment is weighing on top-line growth but easing margin
pressure. We expect NEV to remain a headwind for Inovance given
slowing demand and intense price competition. However, margin
pressure from the rapid expansion of NEV revenues should ease in 2026.
Inovance expects its NEV segment to grow faster than the industry,
driven by new product development and penetration of new overseas
customers.
•
Overseas, the new growth driver. Overseas revenue grew 30.0% YoY in
2025 and accounted for 5.9% of total revenue. Inovance targets overseas
markets to contribute 20–30% of revenue over time. We expect overseas
revenue to become a long-term growth driver for the company.
•
2026 guidance was for revenue and net profit growth of 10-30% YoY.
Earnings changes: We cut our 2026/27E net profits 9%/13% on slower
revenue growth.
Valuation: We cut our TP 10% to Rmb77.80, rolling forward to 30.8x 2027E
P/E multiple (1SD below historical avg PE) on slowing NEV demand, from
36.3x
Catalyst: Industrial automation demand rebound.
Investment Thesis and Recommendation
Inovance is a domestic leader in the industrial automation industry for
servo systems, inverters, PLC and industrial robots. We expect domestic
market-share gains on import substitution and overseas expansion. For
more research, see here.

Key Risks to Investment Thesis
•
Increasing pricing competition in the industrial automation
segment.
•
Slowing down NEV market demand.
•
A slower-than-expected FA market demand recovery.
Company Description
Inovance is a leading provider of industrial automation
solutions. It focuses on development, manufacturing and sales
of industrial automation components (servos/ controllers/
inverters) with applications in industrial robots, automated
manufacturing equipment and EV. With advanced core
technology and savvy expansion, it has managed to deliver
sustainable growth amid rising competition.
