Mao Geping (MGP) attended our China Summit and we met with MGP 
management. We believe MGP management's commitment that no 
share reduction in the short term (6 months) will partially ease investor 
concerns post its recent placement. Meanwhile, MGP maintained focus 
on shareholder returns, including steady dividends (>30% payout ratio) 
and share buybacks (in preparation). We also see solid growth visibility 
for MGP in 2026, supported by its leading positioning in the 
experience-led consumption trend, a clear roadmap to grow its store 
network, increasing brand awareness, solid product offerings tailored to 
Chinese facial features and aesthetics, category expansion, and a 
continuously differentiated retail experience. Our Dec-26 PT of 
HK$130 implies 31x 27E P/E. MGP is our top pick in the China beauty 
segment.
• On track to 2026 guidance (sales +30% with stable margin). 1Q 
sales went up by >20% yoy, with offline +c20% (SSSG + >12%, 
better than the full year guidance), and Apr continued the solid trend. 
This was within MGP's internal expectations and MGP remained 
confident in achieving its 2026 guidance. In terms of channel 
development: 1) Online: MGP adopted an early-start approach with 
campaigns initiated since 1 May for the upcoming 18 Jun events. 
This should help MGP to seize the edge during the event, in our 
view. As a reminder, sales during 18 Jun and 11.11 events accounted 
for c40% of full year online sales. 2) Offline: MGP maintained its 
target of 30 net openings in 2026, mainly in the high-end shopping 
malls in high-tier cites.  
• Overseas expansion strategy. MGP is actively exploring overseas 
markets, with its first Hong Kong store (opened in Oct 2025) as a 
first step. MGP expects more stores in HK in 2026, and potentially 
into Southeast Asia, Europe, etc., mainly through self-operated stores 
in high-end shopping malls. Meanwhile, MGP remained agile in 
M&A opportunities. The strategic partnership with L Catterton 
would also enhance its global expansion visibility
• A clear roadmap to support mid-term growth. 1) Customer base 
expansion: there is significant potential in target customer penetration 
(offline 6.4mn vs target customers of 40mn; online 15.6mn, vs target 
customers of 300-400mn); 2) new product pipelines: e.g., MGP 
launched several new products in 1Q, such as the Radiant 
Revitalizing Multi-Action series ( 琉光赋活系列), Perfection 
Radiance Primer Cream (皮肤衣), UV Primer (防晒隔离乳), etc.; 3) 
store network expansion: MGP targets 600 counters (vs. 445 as of 
2025) in the mid-term, with c30 net openings annually.

Summary Investment Thesis and Valuation
MGP is a rare domestic beauty brand benefiting from 
experience-led consumption in China. With a 25-year history 
and a differentiated consumer experience (one-on-one 
personalized makeup and skincare services), we believe MGP 
has a significant opportunity to grow and double its market 
share in the prestige cosmetics market from the current 2%.

offline presence that boosts penetration, solid product

revenue and earnings CAGRs over 25-28E.

27E. We derive our 8.0% WACC assuming a 3.0% risk-free 
rate, 6% risk premium, 8.4% cost of equity and terminal 
growth rate of 2.0%.

Mao Geping Cosmetics Co., Ltd. - H (Overweight; Price Target:

Investment Thesis 
MGP is a rare domestic beauty brand benefiting from experience-led consumption in 
China. With a 25-year history and a differentiated consumer experience (one-on-one 
personalized makeup and skincare services), we believe MGP has a significant 
opportunity to grow and double its market share in the prestige cosmetics market 
from the current 2%. We believe MGP’s enhanced brand equity, a distinctive offline 
presence that boosts penetration, solid product offerings, and category expansion 
should drive 26%/27% revenue and earnings CAGRs over 25-28E. 
Valuation
Our DCF-based Dec-26 PT of HK$130 implies 31x P/E for 27E. We derive our 8.0% 
WACC assuming a 3.0% risk-free rate, 6% risk premium, 8.4% cost of equity and 
terminal growth rate of 2.0%
