Research Tactical Idea
We believe the share price will  rise  in absolute terms  over the next  60  days.
 This is because the stock has traded off recently, making short term valuation much 
more compelling. Along with its results, ZTO announced that Ms. Di Xu tendered 
her resignation as a non-executive director of the company, effective May 20, 2026, 
given the recent termination of the investor rights agreement with subsidiaries of 
Alibaba Group Holdings Limited. The news appears to have triggered market 
expectations of a  potential Alibaba stake reduction  (9% stake as of end-2025). We 
would view any pullback in the US-listed stock as a good entry point given no 
fundamental concerns behind  the share price correction, and we expect ZTO to 
resume share repurchases soon.  
 We estimate that there is about a 70% to 80% (or "very likely")  probability for the 
scenario.
Estimated probabilities are illustrative and assigned subjectively based on our 
assessment of the likelihood of the scenario.

Valuation Methodology and Risks
 ZTO Express (ZTO.N)  
Discounted cash flow, probability-weighted 15% bull case, 75% base case, 10% bear 
case. Greater skew towards the bull case vs bear case reflects ZTO&#39;s market leadership 
in terms of volume and unit profitability. We see a mild probability that our bear case could 
play out if there are significant disruptions to the competitive landscape from new players 
joining the industry.
Key assumptions: 13.2% WACC and 3% terminal growth rate.
 Risks to Upside  
n Faster-than-expected market share gains
n Better cost control
n Peers' weak performance in terms of volume, revenue growth, or profitability
n Improvement in shareholder returns
 Risks to Downside  
n Continued market share loss
n Industry competition is more intensified than expected
n Market consolidation stagnates
