Valuation Methodology and Risks
 Laopu Gold (6181.HK)  
Base case scenario, P/E-based valuation methodology, consistent with the rest of our con-
sumer discretionary coverage. We use 20x 2026e P/E, which implies 0.5x PEG, lower than the 
average for consumer stocks.
We think this is reasonable considering gold price volatility and macro weakness. In addition, 
considering that growth is likely to decelerate in 2026 on a higher comp base, we believe a 
lower-than-peers PEG assumption is reasonable.
 Risks to Upside  
n More proactive consumption-related policy support
n Faster-than-expected same store sales growth
n Gold price rally
 Risks to Downside  
n Delay in consumption-related policy support
n Weaker-than-expected demand trend due to sharp gold price correction
n Sharp sales growth slowdown
