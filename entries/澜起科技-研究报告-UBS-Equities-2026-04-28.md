Montage Technology - H
GM delivers a strong beat 
Q126 results beat 
Q126 net profit attributable to shareholders was Rmb847mn (+61% YOY/ +40% QoQ), 
21% above our estimate, mainly due to significant GM improvement, prudent R&D 
expense, and one-off gain from the disposal of XConn to Marvell (Rmb239mn), partially 
offset by higher G&A expense (related to stock-based compensation) and FX loss. 
GM  improvement on better product mix
Q126 revenue was Rmb1,461bn, +20% YoY/ +4% QoQ, although the  sequential 
growth was still capped by BT substrate tightness (which has been eased to some extent 
in the past quarter) and CNY/USD appreciation. Of which, interconnects revenue was 
Rmb1,417mn (+24% YoY/ +8% QoQ). GM significantly improved +9.3ppt  YoY and 
+5.3ppt QoQ, mainly due to better product mix: 1) higher contribution from DDR5 Gen3 
and Gen4 RCD, and 2) higher mix of MRCD/MDB, PCIe Retimer, CKD and CXL MXC 
(revenue was Rmb 269mn, +94% YoY, accounting for 19% of total interconnects), 
which carry much higher margins. We believe the solid GM increase reflects Montage's 
technology leadership in memory interconnects and structural demand from AI.
Diverse product portfolio well positioned in AI era
We continue to see Montage as a key beneficiary in rising AI capex, from both memory 
and high-speed connectivity. We expect the earnings growth to be supported by 1) 
rising importance of MRDIMM for agentic AI, 2) higher mix from newer generation RCD 
interconnects, 3) higher adoption of AEC (PCIe Retimer inside to scale up GPU/ASICs), 
and 4) the potential roll-out of CXL among hyperscalers to better utilize memory 
resource.
Valuation: PT and rating under review
Our PT and earnings forecasts are under review.

Company Description
Founded in 2004, Montage is a leader in the memory interconnect chip industry by revenue 
and an innovative pioneer of other interconnect solutions designed to enhance data transfer 
reliability and efficiency in AI servers and PCs. The company is headquartered in Shanghai and 
has branches in Kunshan, Beijing, Xi'an, Macau, the United States and South Korea.
Valuation Method and Risk Statement
We use PE methodology to value Montage Tech-H.
Downside risks include an overall semiconductor industry downcycle, lower-than-expected 
ramp up of server CPU platforms supporting MRDIMM, rising competition in PCIe retimer 
chips, progress in PCIe switches below expectations.
Upside risks include a broader semiconductor industry recovery, higher AI CAPEX from 
Chinese hyper-scalers and telcos, higher penetration of newer-generation DDR5 RCDs, faster 
adoption of MRDIMMs in CPU platforms, breakthroughs in high-speed SerDes, progress in 
PCIe switches and ethernet switches beyond expectations.
