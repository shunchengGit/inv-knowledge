1Q Rev/EPS beat driven by Search and Cloud upside 
1Q Rev/EPS of $94.7bn/$5.11 beat Street $91.7bn/$2.73, driven by upside in Search & 
Cloud.  Search growth accelerated to 19% y/y (vs Street 16%), while Cloud was the big 
surprise at 63% y/y (vs Street 50%). 1Q Op. margin at 41.9% beat Street at 39.6%, with  
Cloud at 32.9% (vs 27.4% est.) suggesting AI cycle returns are trending positively. EPS 
also benefited from $37.7bn in Other Income from unrealized private asset appreciation. 
Positive surprises: Cloud backlog & external TPU sales 
Cloud backlog grew 90% q/q to $462bn, with 50% expected to be recognized over next 
24 months, helping validate infrastructure investments and advantages of Google’s 
Gemini and TPU offerings. Based on backlog on the books ($230bn to be recognized 
over the next 8 quarters), we expect significant increases in Street Cloud revenue 
growth estimates. A portion of the q/q growth represented contracted TPU sales to 
external customers, a new, potentially large, and profitable business for Google.  
Raising 2027 net revenue by 10% and EPS by 12%  
Raising estimates to reflect higher search growth, Cloud backlog and Cloud margins. For 
2026, we raise revenue by 4% to $424bn and EPS by 26% to $14.43. For 2027, we raise 
revenue by 10% to $522bn, EPS by 12% to $14.49.  We also raise 2027 capex by 25% 
(+$70bn y/y) to $257bn, which we think is justified by material backlog expansion.  We 
Raise our PO to $430 based on a higher ’27 EPS & higher 28x multiple (vs 27x) given 
growing mix of recurring Cloud revenue. 
AI strengthening core business and growth outlook; Buy 
Another quarter of strong revenues and margins reinforces our view that Google is a top 
AI play, with increasing search usage, improving ad targeting, durable Cloud advantages 
from Gemini/TPUs, and growing subscription revenues from Gemini.  We think AI 
tailwinds for Search are still in early stages and future LLM improvements will drive 
durable growth. For Cloud, added capacity and TPU sales could further drive upside. At 
AH price of ~$372, stock is at 25x our revised 2027E EPS, and we think search strength 
and Cloud backlog visibility warrant a higher multiple. Next catalysts: I/O Conf (May 19), 
upgraded Gemini model, consumer agentic launches, and new Cloud deals. Reit Buy.
