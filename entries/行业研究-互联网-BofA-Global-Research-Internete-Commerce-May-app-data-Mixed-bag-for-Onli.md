May app data: Mixed bag for Online media, 
delivery decelerates, & AI apps strong 
Industry Overview 
   
 
 
May app trends: big eCommerce consolidates DAUs 
We track monthly global mobile app DAUs and downloads per Sensor Tower to gauge 
user trends across top subsectors including social media, eCommerce, subscription 
services, and travel.  For May, Online Media DAU trends were mixed, with Facebook 
DAUs decelerating and Pinterest accelerating.  DAU growth for Amazon accelerated 
while many smaller platforms slowed, possibly reflecting a shift toward value as inflation 
increases.  Delivery DAUs decelerated, with Uber Eats an exception. AI app DAUs mostly 
slowed, but y/y growth was still strong; ChatGPT was up 68%, Gemini grew 320%, and 
Meta AI was up 1,300%, while Claude growth accelerated to over 1,300%. 
Online Media: Mixed May DAU trends, Pinterest best m/m 
Facebook DAUs contracted 4% y/y in May, a modest deceleration from -2% y/y in Apr, 
while Instagram DAU growth of 4% y/y accelerated 1ppt m/m. YouTube DAUs were 
stable (at flat y/y) and DLs slowed to +2% y/y. Snap DAUs grew a stable 3%, while DLs 
declined 7% (6ppt decel). Pinterest DAUs grew 14% y/y (4ppt accel) and DLs increased 
2% y/y (4ppt decel).  Reddit DAUs declined 1% (stable m/m), and DLs declined 37% (y/y 
comps get easier next month). Roblox DAU growth slowed 4ppts to +9%. Google DAUs 
grew a stable 12% y/y, with DLs declining 8% y/y (an 8ppt decel vs Apr). 
eComm: Amazon, Wayfair, and Carvana outperform m/m 
Amazon DAU growth of 4% y/y in May accelerated 1ppt from Apr, while DL growth of 
4% was stable.  eBay DAUs declined 2% y/y (3ppt decel m/m). Etsy DAUs grew 6% y/y 
(6ppt decel from Apr), while DLs contracted 18% (9ppt decel).  Chewy DAUs grew 17% 
y/y, a 20ppt decel. from Apr, while DL trends slowed 9ppts to -20% y/y.  Wayfair DAUs 
grew 4% y/y, accelerating 8ppts on 21ppt easier comp.  Carvana DAU growth 
accelerated to 24% y/y (from 20% in Apr), also on an easier comp. 
Gig: Uber relatively stable at +10%, Dash/Cart decelerates 
Uber DAUs grew 10% y/y, a modest decel. of 1ppt from April, while DL growth of 10% 
slowed 2ppts.  Lyft DAUs/DLs were -2%/-1% y/y (1ppt accel/decel).  Waymo DAUs 
grew a stable 43% y/y.  DoorDash DAUs growth of 6% y/y slowed 2ppts from Apr, will 
DLs fell 8% y/y, also a 2ppt decel.  Uber Eats DAUs were +3% y/y in May (3ppt accel), 
with DLs up 4% (6ppt accel).  Instacart DAUs declined 5% y/y (4ppt decel) and DLs 
were down 6% y/y (4ppt accel from April). 
Travel & Other: DAU growth for ABNB/BKNG accelerates 
Booking y/y DAUs grew 4% y/y in May (1ppt accel from Apr) while DLs were flat y/y 
(7ppt decel).  Expedia DAUs grew 4% y/y, a 1ppt decel, while DLs declined 33% y/y 
(stable m/m). Airbnb DAUs/DLs grew 11%/30%, an acceleration of 2ppts/7ppts from 
April.  Vrbo DAU growth decelerated 1ppt to 12%. Duolingo DAU growth of 11% 
accel’d 1ppt m/m. StubHub DAUs grew 26% y/y, accelerating 28ppts from Apr, while 
Vivid Seats DAUs declined 3%, decelerating 6ppts m/m.

 Mobile app data update 
Sensor Tower DAU & Download estimates combine App Store & Google Play data 
provided by Publisher & Developer partnerships, as well as signals from the stores to 
backfill non-partner data. Sensor Tower has data sharing partnerships with Publishers & 
Developers that collectively represent 50%+ of all App Store revenues. We have 
aggregated this data to gauge potential user & usage trends. Sensor Tower regularly 
revises their Active User & Download estimates.  Per Sensor Tower, these revisions 
are expected to improve estimate measurements for apps. 
Snap 
Per Sensor Tower, global Snap DAUs were 395mn in May, growing 3.0% y/y and slowing 
a modest 20bps from April.  Since March, DAUs are up 2mn to 395mn. Gross downloads 
declined 7% y/y to 29mn in April, decelerating from -1% y/y in April.
