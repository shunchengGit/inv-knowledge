Zhongji Innolight (A) 
Upbeat 1Q; 1.6T to drive margin upside, 
high visibility beyond ’27; CNY1,100 PO 
Reiterate Rating: BUY | PO: 1,100 CNY | Price: 809.61 CNY   
  
 
 
1Q beat, raising confidence to full-year earnings 
1Q earnings beat BofAe/consensus by 24%/31% at CNY5.7bn (+57% QoQ, +262% YoY). 
Revenue of CNY19.5bn (+47% QoQ, +192% YoY) was 32%/26% above BofAe/consensus, 
proving 1.6T started volume take-off. GM reached 46.1% (+1.6ppt QoQ, +9.4ppt YoY), 
indicating product mix is more than able to offset the component price hike and annual 
price erosion. In our view, the strong results ease market’s concern about component 
supply and strengthened earnings expectation in coming quarters. 
1.6T/SiPh to drive margin; strong procurement capability 
1Q results proved 1.6T/SiPh products have a strong margin profile and 1.6T (SiPh) likely 
have a gross margin of 55-60%. With 1.6T shipment rising sequentially, we expect 
margin to further expand into 2H26. Additionally, prepayment hit CNY1.5bn vs historical 
level of below CNY200, suggesting Innolight is aggressively stocking up core component 
to meet customer demand. 
NPO/XPO demand likely to materialize from 2027 
While key customers have already stated their demand outlook for 2027, some of them 
have also started planning for 2028 with a more diversified product requirement. Mgmt. 
also noted they’ve received concrete NPO orders and should kick-off shipment from 
2027, while customer interests on XPO are also very strong. Visibility remains high and 
we expect growth trajectory to last beyond 2027, backed by Innolight’s comprehensive 
know-how across pluggable, NPO/XPO/CPO and even OCS. 
Reiterate Buy; lift PO to CNY1,100 
We revise up 2026-28E earnings by 23-29% to reflect the results and positive outlook. 
We raise PO to CNY1,100 (30x 2H26-1H27E P/E, unchanged) from CNY888. We reiterate 
Buy on Innolight.

Company Description 
Zhongji Innolight, a China-based high-speed optical 
transceiver solution provider, was founded in 2008 and 
listed in 2017 (via a reverse takeover). Innolight is now tied 
as the largest optical transceiver provider globally. It has a 
complete product portfolio for datacenter, 5G, networking 
and fiber to the home. Its key customers are mainly US 
hyperscalers and GPU providers. 
Investment Rationale 
We have a Buy rating on Zhongji Innolight. Innolight, the 
global number-1 optical transceiver supplier, is a key 
beneficiary of the AI uptrend, led by (1) rapidly rising 
volume/content at transceivers and (2) its strong project 
line-up.
