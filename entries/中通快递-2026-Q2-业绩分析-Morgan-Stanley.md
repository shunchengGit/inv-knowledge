Valuation Methodology and Risks
 ZTO Express (ZTO.N)  
Discounted cash flow, probability-weighted 15% bull case, 75% base case, 10% bear 
case. Greater skew towards the bull case vs bear case reflects ZTO&#39;s market leadership 
in terms of volume and unit profitability. We see a mild probability that our bear case could 
play out if there are significant disruptions to the competitive landscape from new players 
joining the industry.
Key assumptions: 13.2% WACC and 3% terminal growth rate.
 Risks to Upside  
n Faster-than-expected market share gains
n Better cost control
n Peers' weak performance in terms of volume, revenue growth, or profitability
n Improvement in shareholder returns
 Risks to Downside  
n Continued market share loss
n Industry competition is more intensified than expected
n Market consolidation stagnates
